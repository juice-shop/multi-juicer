MultiJuicer deployed! 🎉🥳

MultiJuicer doesn't come with a default way to expose it to the world.
You can find guides for specific cloud providers here: https://github.com/juice-shop/multi-juicer/tree/main/guides

To test it locally run:
$ kubectl port-forward --namespace="{{.Release.Namespace}}" service/multi-juicer 8080:8080
You'll then be able to access MultiJuicer in your browser under http://localhost:8080

To administrate the cluster you can log into MultiJuicer with the admin account:
Username: admin
Password: ${kubectl get secrets multi-juicer-secret --namespace="{{.Release.Namespace}}" -o=jsonpath='{.data.adminPassword}' | base64 --decode}
